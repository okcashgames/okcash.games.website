what is Okcash

About

Okcash is a new and energy friendly cryptocurrency released in 2014. Thanks to its speed and efficacy, works perfect for video games as it presents a faster method of payment working 24/7 while at the same time provides method for rewarding the players. As the players are able to stake the Okcash they win, increasing their rewards and benefits.

OK Cryptocurrency

Okcash is a cryptocurrency, just like Bitcoin, with the main differences of being faster, no need for special hardware to mine for more coins, you just need to place your OK coins in your Okcash wallet, and this will start to mine (mint) for more coins in your behalf.
